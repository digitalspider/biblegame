Scrolls of Life: Bible Game Adventure

This game is an interactive text based RPG game that encourages bible learning in a fun and social way.

Each player has stamina, that they can convert into love, faith, riches, or knowledge, to level up.

Players can also rob and be robbed, however these actions have consequences
